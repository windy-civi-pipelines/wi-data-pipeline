Referred to Committee on Rules.AJR66,1,21Relating to: congratulating Southwest Wisconsin Technical College for winning 2the 2025 Aspen Prize for Community College Excellence.AJR66,1,63Whereas, Southwest Wisconsin Technical College, located in Fennimore, 4Wisconsin, is a part of the Wisconsin Technical College System, a statewide 5network of colleges that provides accessible, affordable, and high-quality education 6and training to support Wisconsin’s workforce and communities; andAJR66,1,107Whereas, the Aspen Institute College Excellence Program awards the 8prestigious Aspen Prize for Community College Excellence to recognize community 9colleges that achieve high and improving levels of student success, particularly for 10students from historically underserved backgrounds; andAJR66,1,1311Whereas, the Aspen Prize is awarded every two years and is based on rigorous 12data analysis, interviews with leadership, faculty, staff, students, and community 13members, and site visits to finalist colleges; andAJR66,2,214Whereas, in 2025, Southwest Wisconsin Technical College was selected as the 1winner of the Aspen Prize for Community College Excellence, earning a $700,000 2award and being named the top two-year college in the country; andAJR66,2,73Whereas, the Aspen Institute College Excellence Program praised Southwest 4Wisconsin Technical College for its robust teaching and learning practices, strong 5relationships with employers and four-year university partners, effective advising, 6and programs that include work-based learning and other high-impact practices;
