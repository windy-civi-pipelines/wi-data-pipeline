Referred to Committee on Transportation and Local Government.SB542,1,31An Act to amend 341.14 (6r) (b) 9. b., 341.14 (6r) (c) and 341.14 (6r) (fm) 7.; to 2create 341.14 (6r) (f) 19g. of the statutes; relating to: special registration 3plates for persons awarded the Global War on Terrorism Service Medal.Analysis by the Legislative Reference BureauThis bill allows persons who have been awarded the Global War on Terrorism Service Medal to obtain special motor vehicle registration plates.Under current law, members of certain designated special groups may obtain special registration plates for certain vehicles that are owned or leased by special group members. A fee, in addition to the regular registration fee for the particular kind of vehicle, is charged for the issuance or reissuance of most special plates.The bill establishes an authorized special group for persons who have been awarded the Global War on Terrorism Service Medal. In addition to the regular vehicle registration fee, special group members are required to pay a $15 fee for issuance or reissuance of the special plates, which is deposited in the veterans trust fund.For further information see the state fiscal estimate, which will be printed as an appendix to this bill.The people of the state of Wisconsin, represented in senate and assembly, do enact as follows:SB542,11Section 1. 341.14 (6r) (b) 9. b. of the statutes is amended to read:SB542,2,62341.14 (6r) (b) 9. b.
