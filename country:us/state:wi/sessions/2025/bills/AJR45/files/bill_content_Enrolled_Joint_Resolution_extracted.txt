andWhereas, the Wauwatosa West Boys Basketball Team won the first state championship in program history, defeating Racine Park 60-57 in a Division II overtime nail-biter; andWhereas, the Wauwatosa West Boys Basketball Team never gave up on themselves and remained mentally tough through the challenges of the season, winning over some crosstown rivals in their pursuit of a first-ever state championship; andWhereas, Wauwatosa West Trojans Coach Christopher Newbauer and the boys basketball team have achieved a well-earned winning season and lasting legacy at Wauwatosa West; now, therefore, be itResolved by the assembly, the senate concurring, That the Wisconsin State Legislature hereby commends and congratulates the Wauwatosa West Boys Basketball Team on their 2025 Division II State Basketball Championship.Senator Mary FelzkowskiPresident of the SenateRepresentative Robin J.
