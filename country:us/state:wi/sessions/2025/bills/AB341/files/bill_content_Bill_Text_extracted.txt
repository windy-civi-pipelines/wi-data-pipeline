Referred to Committee on Education.AB341,1,31An Act to create 20.255 (3) (cm) and 115.429 of the statutes; relating to: 2grants for prospective school social workers, granting rule-making authority, 3and making an appropriation.Analysis by the Legislative Reference BureauUnder this bill, the Department of Public Instruction must establish and administer a grant program to award grants for the costs of obtaining the education required for a school social worker license issued by DPI. The grants may be awarded only to an individual who is a member of a racial minority group and who is either an employee of a school board or enrolled in an undergraduate or graduate program leading to licensure as a school social worker.For further information see the state fiscal estimate, which will be printed as an appendix to this bill.The people of the state of Wisconsin, represented in senate and assembly, do enact as follows:AB341,14Section 1. 20.005 (3) (schedule) of the statutes: at the appropriate place, 5insert the following amounts for the purposes indicated: - See PDF for table AB341,27Section 2. 20.255 (3) (cm) of the statutes is created to read:AB341,2,9820.255 (3) (cm) Grants for prospective school social workers. (1) In this 12section, “member of a racial minority group” means an individual who is any of the 13following:AB341,2,1414(a) A Black American.AB341,2,1515(b) An American Indian or Alaskan native.AB341,2,1616(c) A Hispanic, as defined in s. 16.287 (1) (d).AB341,2,1717(d) An individual of Asian or Pacific Island origin.AB341,2,1818(e) An individual whose ancestry includes 2 or more races.AB341,2,2119(2) The department shall establish and administer a grant program to award 20to individuals who meet the eligibility requirements under sub. (3) grants for the 21costs of acquiring education needed for licensure as a school social worker.AB341,3,222(3) Grants may be awarded under this section only to an individual who is a 1member of a racial minority group and who meets one of the following 2requirements:AB341,3,33(a) The individual is an employee of a school board.AB341,3,64(b) The individual is enrolled in an institution of higher education or an 5accredited graduate school in a program of study leading to licensure as a school 6social worker.AB341,3,77(4) The department may promulgate rules to implement this section.AB341,48Section 4.
