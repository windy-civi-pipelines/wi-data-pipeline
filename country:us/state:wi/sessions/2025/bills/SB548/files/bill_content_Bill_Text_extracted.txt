Referred to Committee on Financial Institutions and Sporting Heritage.SB548,1,21An Act to amend 20.370 (1) (mu); to create 29.063 (7) of the statutes; relating 2to: chronic wasting disease sampling and making an appropriation.Analysis by the Legislative Reference BureauCurrent law allows the Department of Natural Resources to manage and provide funding to conduct testing for chronic wasting disease in cervids, which are any species of deer or elk that are present in the wild and are not farm-raised deer. Currently, DNR administers a program under which it provides chronic wasting disease sampling stations, including self-service kiosks, where hunters may drop off deer carcass parts for sampling. This bill codifies this program by requiring DNR to establish and administer it and provides $1,000,000 in the 2025-27 fiscal biennium for the program. The bill allows DNR to contract with private entities to conduct the sampling and to pay private entities per sample taken.For further information see the state fiscal estimate, which will be printed as an appendix to this bill.The people of the state of Wisconsin, represented in senate and assembly, do enact as follows:SB548,13Section 1. 20.370 (1) (mu) of the statutes is amended to read:SB548,2,10120.370 (1) (mu) General program operations — state funds. The amounts in 2the schedule for general program operations that are conducted under ss. 23.09 to 323.11, 27.01, 30.203, 30.277, and 90.21, and chs. 29 and 169, for activities conducted 4under the ecological inventory and monitoring program of the endangered 5resources program, for the aquatic and terrestrial resources inventory under s. 623.09 (2) (km), for providing the signage required under s. 23.118, for the chronic 7wasting disease sampling station program under s. 29.063 (7), and for payments of 8$53,700 in each fiscal year, to be credited to the appropriation account under s. 920.285 (1) (k), to the University of Wisconsin System for outdoor skills training 10under s. 29.598.SB548,211Section 2. 29.063 (7) of the statutes is created to read:SB548,2,171229.063 (7) The department shall establish and administer a program under 13which it provides, at locations determined by the department, chronic wasting 14disease sampling stations, including self-service kiosks, where hunters may drop off 15deer carcass parts for chronic wasting disease sampling. The department may 16contract with private entities to conduct the sampling and may pay private entities 17per sample taken.SB548,318Section 3.
