andSJR53,1,86Whereas, mental health conditions affect one in five U.S. adults and one in six 7youth annually, touching the lives of Wisconsinites across every age group and 8region; andSJR53,1,129Whereas, Wisconsin is facing a significant rise in youth mental health 10challenges, mirroring a nationwide 31 percent increase in emergency room visits 11related to mental health among young people in 2020, a concerning trend that has 12continued to grow in recent years;
