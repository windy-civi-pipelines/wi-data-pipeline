ASSEMBLY SUBSTITUTE AMENDMENT 2,
TO SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 88
October 14, 2025 - Offered by Representative Penterman.
SJR88-ASA2,1,2
1Relating to: honoring the life and legacy of Charlie Kirk and condemning all forms
2of political violence.
SJR88-ASA2,1,63Whereas, Charlie Kirk dedicated his life with unwavering conviction to
4advancing the timeless principles of faith, liberty, and truth, most notably through
5his founding of Turning Point USA, which boldly equipped and inspired a
6generation of young Americans to defend the values that sustain our nation; and
SJR88-ASA2,1,97Whereas, his powerful voice, seen in his writings, broadcasts, and countless
8speeches, challenged complacency, awakened courage, and emboldened millions to
9take up the cause of freedom with renewed strength and clarity; and
SJR88-ASA2,1,1210Whereas, beyond his public mission, Charlie Kirk was first and foremost a
11devoted husband and loving father, fiercely committed to his wife and their two
12young children, whom he treasured as the heart of his life; and
SJR88-ASA2,2,213Whereas, the brutal and senseless act that claimed his life has shaken our
1hearts, leaving an immense void not only in his family but across our nation, which
2looked to him as a steadfast champion for liberty; and
SJR88-ASA2,2,53Whereas, the Wisconsin Legislature strongly condemns the assassination of
4Charlie Kirk and extends their deepest condolences to his family and loved ones;
5and
SJR88-ASA2,2,76Whereas, democracy in the United States depends on the peaceful and civil
7debate of ideas—not intimidation, violence, or assassination; and
SJR88-ASA2,2,108Whereas, public officials and other public figures across the nation have faced
9an escalating pattern of politically motivated violence, targeting leaders across the
10political spectrum; and
SJR88-ASA2,2,1311Whereas, acts of political violence and threats against elected officials and
12public figures undermine the foundation of our constitutional democracy by chilling
13free speech and impeding civic engagement; and
SJR88-ASA2,2,1514Whereas, threats or acts of violence against public officials and public figures
15degrade the dignity, safety, and integrity of our democratic institutions; and
SJR88-ASA2,2,1916Whereas, we are heartbroken by the assassination of Charlie Kirk, and we are
17praying with urgency and faith for his wife, for his two small children, and for our
18country as we seek God’s strength to endure and overcome this devastating loss;
19now, therefore, be it
SJR88-ASA2,2,2320Resolved by the assembly, the senate concurring, That the Wisconsin
21Legislature honors Charlie Kirk’s extraordinary life and legacy with deepest
22gratitude, recognizing his fearless devotion to God, his unshakable love of family,
23and his tireless service to country; and, be it further
SJR88-ASA2,3,2
1Resolved, That the Wisconsin Legislature commends his memory to future
2generations as a beacon of conviction and courage; and, be it further
SJR88-ASA2,3,53Resolved, That the Wisconsin Legislature joins together in prayer for his
4family and for our nation, that God would bring healing, peace, and resolve as his
5legacy continues to inspire.
SJR88-ASA2,3,66(end)
