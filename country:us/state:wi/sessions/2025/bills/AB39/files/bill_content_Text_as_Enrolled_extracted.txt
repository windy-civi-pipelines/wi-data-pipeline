II of ch. 114 or under ch. 231, 232, 234, 237, 238, or 279.(b) Each state agency shall require employees of that state agency to perform their assigned work duties at the offices of the state agency during the employee’s regularly scheduled work hours for at least 80 percent of the employee’s regularly scheduled work hours per month. This paragraph does not apply to duties that were performed off site, rather than at the offices of the state agency, before March 1, 2020. This paragraph does not apply to employees of the investment board.AB39,2Section 2. Effective date.(1) This act takes effect on December 31, 2025.
