Referred to Committee on Criminal Justice and Public Safety.AB300,1,21An Act to create 165.985 of the statutes; relating to: a grant program for the 2purchase of automated registration plate reader systems.Analysis by the Legislative Reference BureauThis bill establishes a grant program, administered by the Department of Justice, that provides grants to law enforcement agencies to purchase automated registration plate reader systems. Under the bill, to be eligible for a grant, a law enforcement agency must apply for a grant and include in the application a proposed plan of expenditure of the grant moneys. The bill requires DOJ to ensure that at least 50 percent of the grant moneys awarded under the program are awarded to law enforcement agencies located in rural areas.For further information see the state and local fiscal estimate, which will be printed as an appendix to this bill.The people of the state of Wisconsin, represented in senate and assembly, do enact as follows:AB300,13Section 1. 165.985 of the statutes is created to read:AB300,1,54165.985 Automated registration plate reader grant program. (1) In 5this section:AB300,2,31(a) “Automated registration plate reader system” means one or more high-2speed cameras combined with data processing to convert images of registration 3plates and vehicles into computer readable data.AB300,2,104(b) “Law enforcement agency” means an agency of a political subdivision of 5this state or of a federally recognized Indian tribe or band in this state whose 6purpose is the detection and prevention of crime and enforcement of laws or 7ordinances and whose employees are authorized to make arrests for crimes or 8violations of ordinances. “Law enforcement agency” includes the Marquette 9University police department under s. 175.42. “Law enforcement agency” does not 10include an agency of the state.AB300,2,1311(c) “Rural area” means a city, town, or village in this state that has a 12population of less than 20,000 and that is at least 15 miles from any city, town, or 13village that has a population of at least 20,000.AB300,2,1814(2) The department of justice shall award grants to law enforcement agencies 15to purchase automated registration plate reader systems to be used for public safety 16and law enforcement purposes. The department of justice shall ensure that at least 1750 percent of the grant moneys awarded under this section are awarded to law 18enforcement agencies located in rural areas.AB300,2,2219(3) A law enforcement agency may apply to the department of justice for a 20grant under this section and shall include a proposed plan of expenditure of the 21grant moneys. The department of justice shall review each application and plan 22and may provide a grant under this section to any law enforcement agency.AB300,2,2323(end) Down Down /2025/related/proposals/ab300 true proposaltext /2025/related/proposals/ab300 proposaltext/2025/REG/AB300 proposaltext/2025/REG/AB300 section true Menu»2025»Related Documents»Proposal Text»AB300: Bill Text × Details for PDF view Link (Permanent link) Bookmark this location View toggle Go to top of document Search in this chapter Search in this section Search in this agency Search in this chapter group Search in this chapter Search in this section Cross references for section Acts affecting this section References to this 1970 Statutes Annotations Appellate Court Citations Administrative Code Index Reference lines Clear highlighting
