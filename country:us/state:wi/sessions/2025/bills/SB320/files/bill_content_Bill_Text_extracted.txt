Referred to Committee on Education.SB320,1,31An Act to create 20.255 (2) (fi) and 115.403 of the statutes; relating to: grants 2for LGBTQIA+ rights training for school counselors and school social workers 3and making an appropriation.Analysis by the Legislative Reference BureauUnder this bill, the Department of Public Instruction must award grants to school boards to provide training in LGBTQIA+ rights, in a program developed or approved by DPI, to school counselors and school social workers employed by school boards.
