Referred to Committee on Licensing, Regulatory Reform, State and Federal Affairs.SB571,1,21An Act to amend 1.10 (4); to create 1.10 (3) (w) of the statutes; relating to: 2designating the hen-of-the-woods as the Wisconsin state mushroom.Analysis by the Legislative Reference BureauCurrent law designates a variety of state symbols and requires the Wisconsin Blue Book to include information about them.
