Referred to Committee on Rules.AJR64,1,11Relating to: designating June 2025 as Myasthenia Gravis Awareness Month.AJR64,1,42Whereas, myasthenia gravis (MG) is an autoimmune neuromuscular disease 3that causes chronic weakness, in which antibodies attack receptors in the body and 4prevent muscle contraction; andAJR64,1,85Whereas, MG frequently weakens muscles that control eye movement, facial 6expressions, and swallowing, and its symptoms may include blurred vision, speech 7impairment, difficulty chewing, shortness of breath, an unstable gait, and a 8weakened grip; andAJR64,1,109Whereas, a diagnosis of MG may be missed or delayed because it shares 10symptoms in common with many other disorders; andAJR64,1,1411Whereas, in some cases, MG symptoms can worsen to the point of disability, 12grave illness, or death if there are gaps in or an absence of the several crucial health 13care treatments that can enable those who endure MG to lead productive, active 14lives of normal longevity; andAJR64,2,21Whereas, MG is found most commonly in adult women under 40 and men over 260 but is not specific to either gender or any particular ethnic background; andAJR64,2,33Whereas, MG is nonhereditary and noncontagious; andAJR64,2,54Whereas, 10 to 20 out of every 100,000 Americans are diagnosed with MG, 5and support groups exist in Wisconsin for those people; andAJR64,2,86Whereas, MG Awareness Month is observed every year in June across the 7United States, which allows people from the MG community to come together and 8raise awareness about MG; now, therefore, be itAJR64,2,109Resolved by the assembly, the senate concurring, That June 2025 is 10hereby designated Myasthenia Gravis Awareness Month in Wisconsin.AJR64,2,1111(end) Down Down /2025/related/proposals/ajr64 true proposaltext /2025/related/proposals/ajr64 proposaltext/2025/REG/AJR64 proposaltext/2025/REG/AJR64 section true Menu»2025»Related Documents»Proposal Text»AJR64: Joint Resolution Text × Details for PDF view Link (Permanent link) Bookmark this location View toggle Go to top of document Search in this chapter Search in this section Search in this agency Search in this chapter group Search in this chapter Search in this section Cross references for section Acts affecting this section References to this 1970 Statutes Annotations Appellate Court Citations Administrative Code Index Reference lines Clear highlighting
