Referred to Committee on Licensing, Regulatory Reform, State and Federal Affairs.SB530,1,21An Act to repeal 66.0144; to amend 59.52 (25) of the statutes; relating to: 2eliminating the advisory referenda restrictions under 2023 Wisconsin Act 12.Analysis by the Legislative Reference BureauUnder current law, a county may not conduct a countywide advisory referendum except for an advisory referendum regarding a political subdivision revenue sharing agreement or capital expenditures proposed to be funded by the county property tax levy. In addition, current law prohibits a municipality from conducting an advisory referendum except for an advisory referendum regarding tax incremental financing, a local government telecommunications utility, or capital expenditures proposed to be funded by the municipality’s property tax levy.This bill eliminates the above restrictions and allows a county or municipality to conduct advisory referenda for any purpose, which a county or municipality was allowed to do prior to enactment of 2023 Wisconsin Act 12.The people of the state of Wisconsin, represented in senate and assembly, do enact as follows:SB530,13Section 1. 59.52 (25) of the statutes is amended to read:SB530,2,6459.52 (25) Advisory and contingent referenda. The board may conduct a 1countywide referendum for advisory purposes or for the purpose of ratifying or 2validating a resolution adopted or ordinance enacted by the board contingent upon 3approval in the referendum. The board may not conduct a referendum for advisory 4purposes, except as provided under s. 66.0305 (6) or for an advisory referendum 5regarding capital expenditures proposed to be funded by the county property tax 6levy.SB530,27Section 2. 66.0144 of the statutes is repealed.SB530,2,88(end) Down Down /2025/related/proposals/sb530 true proposaltext /2025/related/proposals/sb530 proposaltext/2025/REG/SB530 proposaltext/2025/REG/SB530 section true Menu»2025»Related Documents»Proposal Text»SB530: Bill Text × Details for PDF view Link (Permanent link) Bookmark this location View toggle Go to top of document Search in this chapter Search in this section Search in this agency Search in this chapter group Search in this chapter Search in this section Cross references for section Acts affecting this section References to this 1970 Statutes Annotations Appellate Court Citations Administrative Code Index Reference lines Clear highlighting
