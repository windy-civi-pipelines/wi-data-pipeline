SB203-SA1,2,136“632.865 (4m) Prompt payment required. A pharmacy benefit manager
7shall remit payment for a claim to a pharmacy or pharmacist within 30 days from
8the day that the claim is received by the pharmacy benefit manager if the claim is
9submitted electronically to the pharmacy benefit manager by the pharmacy or
10pharmacist. A pharmacy benefit manager shall remit payment for a claim to a
11pharmacy or pharmacist within 60 days from the day that the claim is received by
12the pharmacy benefit manager if the claim is submitted by paper to the pharmacy
13benefit manager by the pharmacy or pharmacist.”.

SB203-SA1,2,1715“10. Require that a claim for a drug include any identification, billing

SB203-SA1,2,2119“(d) The pharmacy benefit manager may not condition participation in a

SB203-SA1,3,222(e) The pharmacy benefit manager may not penalize a pharmacy or
