andWhereas, our children need emotional and parental support from both parents to be as successful as possible; andWhereas, separated parents who choose to share parenting duties significantly decrease the chances of undesirable youth troubles and struggles; andWhereas, shared parenting is shown to have a significantly positive effect on children’s well-being; andWhereas, Wisconsin is currently one of the leading states in shared parenting outcomes for children with separated parents; andWhereas, increasing public awareness of the importance of providing children with the parental support they need from both parents benefits Wisconsin’s children, their parents, and all of Wisconsin’s citizens; now, therefore, be itResolved by the senate, the assembly concurring, That the members of the Wisconsin Legislature hereby proclaim April 26, 2025, as Shared Parenting Day in Wisconsin.Representative Robin J.
