Referred to Committee on Licensing, Regulatory Reform, State and Federal Affairs.SB520,1,31An Act to renumber 11.1208 (4); to create 11.1208 (4) (b) of the statutes; 2relating to: expressly prohibiting a foreign national from making a 3contribution to a referendum committee.Analysis by the Legislative Reference BureauCurrent law provides that no person may make or accept a contribution, make a disbursement, make an expenditure, or incur an obligation in violation of the federal law that prohibits a foreign national from making a contribution in connection with any federal, state, or local election. This bill provides, in addition, that no foreign national may make a contribution to a referendum committee and no referendum committee may accept a contribution from a foreign national. A recent decision by the Federal Election Commission allowed a foreign national to make a contribution to a ballot initiative committee because the FEC determined that a ballot initiative is not an “election” for purposes of the federal law prohibiting contributions from foreign nationals.The people of the state of Wisconsin, represented in senate and assembly, do enact as follows:SB520,14Section 1. 11.1208 (4) of the statutes is renumbered 11.1208 (4) (a).SB520,21Section 2. 11.1208 (4) (b) of the statutes is created to read:SB520,2,5211.1208 (4) (b) No foreign national may make a contribution to a referendum 3committee, and no referendum committee may accept a contribution from a foreign 4national. In this paragraph, “foreign national” has the meaning given in 11 CFR 5110.20 (a) (3) and 52 USC 30121 (b).SB520,2,66(end) Down Down /2025/related/proposals/sb520 true proposaltext /2025/related/proposals/sb520 proposaltext/2025/REG/SB520 proposaltext/2025/REG/SB520 section true Menu»2025»Related Documents»Proposal Text»SB520: Bill Text × Details for PDF view Link (Permanent link) Bookmark this location View toggle Go to top of document Search in this chapter Search in this section Search in this agency Search in this chapter group Search in this chapter Search in this section Cross references for section Acts affecting this section References to this 1970 Statutes Annotations Appellate Court Citations Administrative Code Index Reference lines Clear highlighting
