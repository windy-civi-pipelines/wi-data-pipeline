Referred to Committee on Judiciary and Public Safety.SB394,1,21An Act to amend 943.01 (2) (e) and 943.017 (2) (e) of the statutes; relating to: 2damage or graffiti to certain historical property and providing a penalty.Analysis by the Legislative Reference BureauUnder current law, it is a Class I felony to damage or graffiti certain vehicles, highways, common carrier property, grand juror property, and state-owned historical property. This bill makes it a Class I felony to damage or graffiti any structure, plaque, statue, painting, or other monument of commemorative or historical significance that is on public property or is maintained by the state or any county or municipality.Because this bill creates a new crime or revises a penalty for an existing crime, the Joint Review Committee on Criminal Penalties may be requested to prepare a report.The people of the state of Wisconsin, represented in senate and assembly, do enact as follows:SB394,13Section 1. 943.01 (2) (e) of the statutes is amended to read:SB394,2,24943.01 (2) (e) The property damaged is on state-owned publicly owned land or 5is maintained by the state or any county or municipality and either is listed on the 1registry under sub.
