andSJR41,1,76Whereas, 2025 also marks the 50th anniversary of the Fall of Phnom Penh in 7Cambodia in 1975; andSJR41,1,118Whereas, in the aftermath of these events, a substantial number of 9individuals of diverse ethnicity from Cambodia, Laos, and Vietnam sought refuge 10and resettled in the United States, enriching our nation with their diverse cultures, 11traditions, and contributions; andSJR41,1,1412Whereas, such communities largely resettled in Wisconsin, overcoming 13adversity to establish vibrant communities that have significantly contributed to 14the social, cultural, and economic fabric of our state;
