Referred to Committee on Forestry, Parks and Outdoor Recreation.AB416,1,31An Act to amend 31.385 (2) (d); to create 31.385 (2) (dr) and 31.385 (8) of the 2statutes; relating to: a grant for modernization of a dam in the village of 3Rothschild and making an appropriation.Analysis by the Legislative Reference BureauUnder 2025 Act 15, the biennial budget bill, the Joint Committee on Finance was appropriated $42,000,000 to supplement an appropriation to the Department of Natural Resources for providing financial assistance for the modernization of a dam on the Wisconsin River in the village of Rothschild.This bill requires DNR to provide $42,000,000 to Domtar Paper Company, LLC, (Domtar) for modernization of a dam associated with the company’s mill in the village of Rothschild. The bill provides that, if Domtar does not commence the dam modernization project by December 31, 2026, it must return to the state the full amount of the financial assistance Domtar received from the state. The bill also provides that, if Domtar sells either its mill in the village of Rothschild or its mill in the city of Nekoosa prior to the completion of the dam modernization project, Domtar must return to the state any unexpended amount of the financial assistance Domtar received from the state.The bill also provides that, if the total amount of financial assistance for the dam modernization project that Domtar receives from all sources exceeds two-thirds of the cost of the project, Domtar must return to the state the difference between two-thirds of the cost of the project and the total amount of financial assistance Domtar received for the project.Finally, the bill also provides that Domtar must agree to invest a total of $100,000,000 in north central Wisconsin via its manufacturing facilities in the village of Rothschild and the city of Nekoosa within three years and to use roundwood and wood chips only from Wisconsin producers to produce products at its facilities in the village of Rothschild. If Domtar fails to comply with either requirement, they must return to the state the full amount of financial assistance Domtar received from the state.For further information see the state fiscal estimate, which will be printed as an appendix to this bill.The people of the state of Wisconsin, represented in senate and assembly, do enact as follows:AB416,11Section 1. 31.385 (2) (d) of the statutes is amended to read:AB416,2,5231.385 (2) (d) The financial assistance that is provided under this section 3shall be paid from the appropriations under ss. 20.370 (4) (ja) and 20.866 (2) (tL) 4and (tx), except as provided in par. pars. The department 15may not require Domtar Paper Company, LLC, to provide documentation of actual 16expenses prior to making the payment under this paragraph.AB416,3,31(b) If the dam modernization project under this subsection is not commenced 2by December 31, 2026, Domtar Paper Company, LLC, shall pay to the department, 3in a manner prescribed by the department, the full amount provided under par. If Domtar Paper 4Company, LLC, produces products at any facility owned or operated by Domtar 5Paper Company, LLC, in the village of Rothschild using roundwood or wood chips 6from non-Wisconsin producers during the duration of the dam modernization 7project under this subsection, Domtar Paper Company, LLC, shall pay to the 8department, in a manner prescribed by the department, the full amount provided 9under par. (a).
