Referred to Committee on Judiciary and Public Safety.SB141,1,31An Act to amend 343.45 (3) and 343.46 (4); to create 343.45 (2m) and 343.46 2(2m) of the statutes; relating to: allowing an unlicensed person to use a motor 3vehicle and providing a penalty.Analysis by the Legislative Reference BureauUnder current law, a person who owns or is in control of a motor vehicle is prohibited from allowing another person to operate the vehicle if the vehicle operator is not authorized to operate a motor vehicle. Current law also prohibits a person from renting a motor vehicle, trailer, or semitrailer to another person who is not authorized to operate a motor vehicle. A violation of either prohibition is a forfeiture of not more than $100.This bill increases the penalty for both violations to a fine not to exceed $1,000 for a first offense and not to exceed $10,000 for a second or subsequent offense. The bill also provides that the prohibitions apply irrespective of whether the person permitting the operation of a motor vehicle had actual knowledge of the vehicle operator’s authorization to operate a motor vehicle.Because this bill creates a new crime or revises a penalty for an existing crime, the Joint Review Committee on Criminal Penalties may be requested to prepare a report.The people of the state of Wisconsin, represented in senate and assembly, do enact as follows:SB141,11Section 1. 343.45 (2m) of the statutes is created to read:SB141,2,42343.45 (2m) Subsections (1) and (2) apply irrespective of whether the person 3authorizing or permitting the operation of a motor vehicle had actual knowledge of 4the vehicle operator’s authorization under this chapter to operate a motor vehicle.SB141,25Section 2. 343.45 (3) of the statutes is amended to read:SB141,2,96343.45 (3) Except as another penalty is provided by s. 343.245 (4) (b), any 7person violating this section may be required to forfeit not more than $100 is subject 8to a fine not to exceed $1,000 for a first offense and not to exceed $10,000 for a 2nd 9or subsequent offense.SB141,310Section 3. 343.46 (2m) of the statutes is created to read:SB141,2,1311343.46 (2m) Subsection (1) applies irrespective of whether the person renting 12a motor vehicle, trailer, or semitrailer to another had actual knowledge of the 13vehicle operator’s authorization under this chapter to operate a motor vehicle.SB141,414Section 4. 343.46 (4) of the statutes is amended to read:SB141,2,1715343.46 (4) Any person violating this section may be required to forfeit not 16more than $100 is subject to a fine not to exceed $1,000 for a first offense and not to 17exceed $10,000 for a 2nd or subsequent offense.SB141,518Section 5.
