Referred to Committee on State Affairs.AB396,1,31An Act to amend 13.124 (1) (b), 13.124 (2) (b) and 13.124 (3) (b); to create 213.124 (4) of the statutes; relating to: appointment of legal counsel by the 3legislature.Analysis by the Legislative Reference BureauCurrent law authorizes the speaker of the assembly, the senate majority leader, and the cochairpersons of the Joint Committee on Legislative Organization to retain legal counsel for a number of purposes. These purposes include retention of legal counsel for matters affecting the interests of the assembly, senate, or legislature. Also, by longstanding practice and constitutional authority, each house, or the houses jointly, may retain legal counsel by action of the full body or bodies or by action of the Committee on Assembly Organization, the Committee on Senate Organization, or JCLO.This bill provides that the assembly, the senate, and the houses jointly, and any legislator or legislative committee, may not obtain legal counsel other than from the Department of Justice unless that action is submitted to the full house, or houses in the case of a joint appointment, and the appointment of legal counsel is approved by a majority of the members present and voting of the house or houses. The people of the state of Wisconsin, represented in senate and assembly, do enact as follows:AB396,11Section 1. 13.124 (1) (b) of the statutes is amended to read:AB396,2,7213.124 (1) (b) The Subject to sub. (4), the speaker of the assembly, in his or her 3sole discretion, may obtain legal counsel other than from the department of justice, 4with the cost of representation paid from the appropriation under s. 20.765 (1) (a), 5in any action in which the assembly is a party or in which the interests of the 6assembly are affected, as determined by the speaker. The speaker shall approve all 7financial costs and terms of representation.AB396,28Section 2. 13.124 (2) (b) of the statutes is amended to read:AB396,2,14913.124 (2) (b) The Subject to sub. The senate majority 14leader shall approve all financial costs and terms of representation.AB396,315Section 3. 13.124 (3) (b) of the statutes is amended to read:AB396,2,221613.124 (3) (b) The Subject to sub. (4), the cochairpersons of the joint 17committee on legislative organization, in their sole discretion, may obtain legal 18counsel other than from the department of justice, with the cost of representation 19paid from the appropriation under s. 20.765 (1) (a) or (b), as determined by the 20cochairpersons, in any action in which the legislature is a party or in which the 21interests of the legislature are affected, as determined by the cochairpersons.
