Referred to Committee on Senate Organization.SJR83,1,21Relating to: recognizing November 10, 2025, as the 50th anniversary of the tragic 2loss of the crew of SS Edmund Fitzgerald.SJR83,1,43Whereas, SS Edmund Fitzgerald, a bulk cargo vessel built by Great Lakes 4Engineering Works, was launched on June 8, 1958, in River Rouge, Michigan; andSJR83,1,65Whereas, the Edmund Fitzgerald measured in at 729 feet and 13,632 gross 6tons, making it the largest ship on the Great Lakes for the following 13 years; andSJR83,1,87Whereas, the ship’s crew unknowingly started their final voyage on November 89, 1975, departing from Superior, Wisconsin; andSJR83,1,109Whereas, the freighter was headed to Zug Island, Michigan, carrying just over 1026,000 tons of iron ore; andSJR83,1,1211Whereas, the weather intensified in the region, causing a violent storm on 12Lake Superior with gale-force winds and heavy seas; andSJR83,2,213Whereas, on November 10, 1975, Captain Ernest McSorley of the Edmund 1Fitzgerald reported problems with his ship, including a loss of radar functionality, 2topside damage, and a starboard list; andSJR83,2,53Whereas, shortly after 7 p.m., Captain McSorley made his last transmission to 4a nearby vessel, stating that they were holding their own before disappearing from 5radar detection; andSJR83,2,86Whereas, SS Arthur M. Anderson and SS William Clay Ford left the refuge of 7Whitefish Bay late that evening in a heroic effort to search for possible survivors of 8the missing ship; andSJR83,2,119Whereas, the Edmund Fitzgerald was found on the bottom of Lake Superior 10the next week, 17 miles short of Whitefish Point, taking with it the lives of all 29 11men onboard; andSJR83,2,1412Whereas, eight of these men were Wisconsin residents, with seven calling the 13south shore of Lake Superior their home and the other living near Lake Michigan; 14andSJR83,2,1615Whereas, despite the dangers, thousands of men and women continue to serve 16in the Great Lakes maritime industry with quiet bravery; now, therefore, be itSJR83,2,2117Resolved by the senate, the assembly concurring, That the members of 18the Wisconsin Legislature solemnly recognize November 10, 2025, as the 50th 19anniversary of the tragic loss of the crew of SS Edmund Fitzgerald and acknowledge 20the family members and friends who continue to be affected by the absence of their 21loved ones.SJR83,2,2222(end) Down Down /2025/related/proposals/sjr83 true proposaltext /2025/related/proposals/sjr83 proposaltext/2025/REG/SJR83 proposaltext/2025/REG/SJR83 section true Menu»2025»Related Documents»Proposal Text»SJR83: Joint Resolution Text × Details for PDF view Link (Permanent link) Bookmark this location View toggle Go to top of document Search in this chapter Search in this section Search in this agency Search in this chapter group Search in this chapter Search in this section Cross references for section Acts affecting this section References to this 1970 Statutes Annotations Appellate Court Citations Administrative Code Index Reference lines Clear highlighting
