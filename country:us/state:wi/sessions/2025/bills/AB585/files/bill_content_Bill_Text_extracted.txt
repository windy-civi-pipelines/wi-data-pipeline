Referred to Committee on Sporting Heritage. ***AUTHORS SUBJECT TO CHANGE***AB585,1,31An Act to amend 1.10 (4); to create 1.10 (3) (w) and (x) of the statutes; 2relating to: designating the wild turkey as the state game bird and the ruffed 3grouse as the state upland game bird.Analysis by the Legislative Reference BureauCurrent law designates a variety of state symbols and requires the Wisconsin Blue Book to include information about them. This bill designates the wild turkey as the Wisconsin state game bird and the ruffed grouse as the Wisconsin state upland game bird and requires the Wisconsin Blue Book to include that information.The people of the state of Wisconsin, represented in senate and assembly, do enact as follows:AB585,14Section 1. 1.10 (3) (w) and (x) of the statutes are created to read:AB585,1,551.10 (3) (w) The wild turkey (meleagris gallopavo) is the state game bird.AB585,1,66(x) The ruffed grouse (bonasa umbellus) is the state upland game bird.AB585,27Section 2. 1.10 (4) of the statutes is amended to read:AB585,2,511.10 (4) The Wisconsin Blue Book shall include the information contained in 2this section concerning the state song, ballad, waltz, dance, beverage, tree, grain, 3flower, bird, fish, animal, domestic animal, wildlife animal, dog, insect, fossil, 4mineral, rock, soil, fruit, tartan, pastry, dairy product, and herb, game bird, and 5upland game bird.AB585,2,66(end) Down Down /2025/related/proposals/ab585 true proposaltext /2025/related/proposals/ab585 proposaltext/2025/REG/AB585 proposaltext/2025/REG/AB585 section true Menu»2025»Related Documents»Proposal Text»AB585: Bill Text × Details for PDF view Link (Permanent link) Bookmark this location View toggle Go to top of document Search in this chapter Search in this section Search in this agency Search in this chapter group Search in this chapter Search in this section Cross references for section Acts affecting this section References to this 1970 Statutes Annotations Appellate Court Citations Administrative Code Index Reference lines Clear highlighting
