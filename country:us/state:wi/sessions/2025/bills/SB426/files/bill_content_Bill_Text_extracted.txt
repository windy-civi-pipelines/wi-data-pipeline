Referred to Committee on Transportation and Local Government.SB426,1,21An Act to amend 341.25 (1) (gm) of the statutes; relating to: registration of 2trailers used for transporting manufactured housing units.Analysis by the Legislative Reference BureauUnder current law, a person may apply to the Department of Transportation for registration as a transporter of modular or other portable housing. DOT may register an eligible applicant upon payment of the applicable fee and issue the applicant a registration plate valid during the calendar year in which it is issued. The person to whom the plate is issued may use the plate on any trailer or temporary undercarriage they own, but only for transporting a modular housing unit or other portable building. This bill expands this registration option to include the transport of manufactured housing units.For further information see the state fiscal estimate, which will be printed as an appendix to this bill.The people of the state of Wisconsin, represented in senate and assembly, do enact as follows:SB426,13Section 1. 341.25 (1) (gm) of the statutes is amended to read:SB426,2,54341.25 (1) (gm) 1.
