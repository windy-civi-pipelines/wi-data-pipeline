(e) 1. a., 8the board shall give a class 1 notice under ch. 985 before it contracts for the work or 9shall contract with a person qualified as a bidder under s. 66.0901 (2).SB216-SSA1,2,14102. This subsection does not apply to public construction if the materials for 16such a the project are donated or if the labor for such a the project is provided by 17volunteers.SB216-SSA1,2,2018(d) This subsection does not apply to highway contracts which the county 19highway committee or the county highway commissioner is authorized by law to let 20or make, which shall be let or made under s. 83.04.SB216-SSA1,221Section 2. 59.52 (29) (c) 2. of the statutes is created to read:SB216-SSA1,3,22259.52 (29) (c) 2. This subsection does not apply to the construction by a private 1person of an improvement that is donated to the county after the completion of 2construction.SB216-SSA1,33Section 3. 59.52 (29) (e) of the statutes is created to read:SB216-SSA1,3,5459.52 (29) (e) 1. Beginning on the effective date of this subdivision .... [LRB 5inserts date], the thresholds under par. (a) are the following:SB216-SSA1,3,66a. For general public works contract bidding, $50,000.SB216-SSA1,3,77b. For general public works contract notice, $10,000.SB216-SSA1,3,1282. On the 5th year commencing after the effective date of this subdivision .... 9[LRB inserts date], and each subsequent 5th year, the thresholds under subd. 1. 10shall be increased by a percentage equal to the percentage change in the U.S. 11consumer price index for the previous 5 years, as determined by the federal 12department of labor, rounded to the nearest multiple of $1,000.SB216-SSA1,413Section 4. 60.47 (1) (a) of the statutes is renumbered 60.47 (1) (am) and 14amended to read:SB216-SSA1,3,171560.47 (1) (am) “Public contract” means a contract for the construction, 16execution, repair, remodeling or improvement of any public work or building or for 17the furnishing of materials or supplies, with an estimated cost greater than $5,000.SB216-SSA1,518Section 5. 60.47 (1) (ag) of the statutes is created to read:SB216-SSA1,3,201960.47 (1) (ag) “Highway” means a public way or thoroughfare, including any 20bridges on the public way or thoroughfare.SB216-SSA1,621Section 6. 60.47 (1) (as) of the statutes is created to read:SB216-SSA1,3,232260.47 (1) (as) “Public highway contract” means a contract for the 23construction, execution, repair, remodeling, or improvement of a highway.SB216-SSA1,724Section 7. 60.47 (2) (a) of the statutes is amended to read:SB216-SSA1,4,5160.47 (2) (a) No town may enter into a public contract with an estimated cost 2of more than $5,000 the notice threshold under sub. (2s) (a) 2. but not more than 3$25,000 the bidding threshold under sub. (2s) (a) 1. unless the town board, or a town 4official or employee designated by the town board, gives a class 1 notice under ch. 5985 before execution of that public contract.SB216-SSA1,86Section 8. 60.47 (2) (b) of the statutes is amended to read:SB216-SSA1,4,11760.47 (2) (b) No town may enter into a public contract with a value of more 8than $25,000 the bidding threshold under sub. (2s) (a) 1. unless the town board, or 9a town official or employee designated by the town board, advertises for proposals to 10perform the terms of the public contract by publishing a class 2 notice under ch. 11985. The town board may provide for additional means of advertising for bids.SB216-SSA1,912Section 9. 60.47 (2m) of the statutes is created to read:SB216-SSA1,5,21360.47 (2m) Public highway contracts. Notwithstanding sub. (2), and 14except as provided in subs. (4) and (5), no town may enter into a public highway 15contract with an estimated cost of more than the notice threshold under sub. (2s) (a) 164. but not more than the bidding threshold under sub. (2s) (a) 3. unless the town 17board, or a town official or employee designated by the town board, gives a class 1 18notice under ch. 985 before execution of that public highway contract. 19Notwithstanding sub. (2), and except as provided in subs. (4) and (5), no town may 20enter into a public highway contract with a value of more than the bidding 21threshold under sub. (2s) (a) 3., unless the town board, or a town official or employee 22designated by the town board, advertises for proposals to perform the terms of the 1public highway contract by publishing a class 2 notice under ch. 985. The town 2board may provide for additional means of advertising for bids.SB216-SSA1,103Section 10. 60.47 (2s) of the statutes is created to read:SB216-SSA1,5,6460.47 (2s) Threshold amounts. (a) Beginning on the effective date of this 5paragraph .... [LRB inserts date], the thresholds under subs. (2) and (2m) are the 6following:SB216-SSA1,5,771. For general public contract bidding, $50,000.SB216-SSA1,5,882. For general public contract notice, $10,000.SB216-SSA1,5,993. For public highway contract bidding, $25,000.SB216-SSA1,5,10104. (a) 13shall be increased by a percentage equal to the percentage change in the U.S. 14consumer price index for the previous 5 years, as determined by the federal 15department of labor, rounded to the nearest multiple of $1,000.SB216-SSA1,1116Section 11. 60.47 (3) of the statutes is amended to read:SB216-SSA1,5,211760.47 (3) Contracts to lowest responsible bidder. The town board shall 18let a public contract or public highway contract, for which advertising for proposals 19is required under sub. (2) (b) or (2m) to the lowest responsible bidder. This section does not 1apply to public contracts or public highway contracts entered into by a town with a 2municipality, as defined under s. 66.0301 (1) (a).SB216-SSA1,133Section 13. 60.47 (5) (title) of the statutes is amended to read:SB216-SSA1,6,5460.47 (5) (title) Exception for emergencies and donated materials and 5labor certain donations.SB216-SSA1,146Section 14. 60.47 (5) of the statutes is renumbered 60.47 (5) (a) and 7amended to read:SB216-SSA1,6,13860.47 (5) (a) This section is optional with respect to public contracts and 9public highway contracts for the repair and construction of public facilities when 10damage or threatened damage to the facility creates an emergency, as declared by 11resolution of the town board, that endangers the public health or welfare of the 12town. This subsection no longer applies when the town board declares that the 13emergency no longer exists.SB216-SSA1,6,1714(b) This section is optional with respect to a public contract or public highway 15contract if the materials related to the contract are donated or if the labor that is 16necessary to execute the public contract or public highway contract is provided by 17volunteers.SB216-SSA1,1518Section 15. 60.47 (5) (c) of the statutes is created to read:SB216-SSA1,6,211960.47 (5) (c) This section does not apply to the construction by a private 20person of an improvement that is donated to the town after the completion of 21construction.SB216-SSA1,1622Section 16. 62.15 (1) of the statutes is renumbered 62.15 (1) (a) and 23amended to read:SB216-SSA1,7,12162.15 (1) (a) All public construction, the estimated cost of which exceeds 2$25,000 the bidding threshold under par. (c) 1. a., the board of public works shall give a class 1 notice, 7under ch. 985, of the proposed construction before the contract for the construction 8is executed. This provision paragraph does not apply to public construction if the 9materials for such a the project are donated or if the labor for such a the project is 10provided by volunteers. The council may also by a vote of three-fourths of all the 11members-elect provide by ordinance that any class of public construction or any 12part thereof may be done directly by the city without submitting the same for bids.SB216-SSA1,1713Section 17. 62.15 (1) (b) of the statutes is created to read:SB216-SSA1,7,141462.15 (1) (b) 1. In this paragraph:SB216-SSA1,7,1615a. “Highway” means a public way or thoroughfare, including any bridges on 16the public way or thoroughfare.SB216-SSA1,7,1817b. “Public highway construction” means a public construction project 18involving the construction, improvement, repair, or maintenance of a highway.
