Referred to Committee on Criminal Justice and Public Safety.AB298,1,31An Act to repeal 165.88 (3m) (d); to amend 165.88 (4); to create 165.88 (3t) of 2the statutes; relating to: grants for technical colleges to provide mapping 3data to law enforcement.Analysis by the Legislative Reference BureauUnder current law, the Department of Justice awards grants to school boards and governing bodies of private schools to assist them in submitting interactive critical mapping data for each school building and facility in the district to law enforcement agencies and the Office of School Safety in DOJ. This bill requires OSS to award grants to technical college district boards so they may submit interactive critical mapping data for each of their technical college buildings to law enforcement agencies and OSS.For further information see the state fiscal estimate, which will be printed as an appendix to this bill.The people of the state of Wisconsin, represented in senate and assembly, do enact as follows:AB298,14Section 1. 165.88 (3m) (d) of the statutes is repealed.AB298,25Section 2. 165.88 (3t) of the statutes is created to read:AB298,2,21165.88 (3t) Grants to technical colleges for interactive critical 2mapping data. (a) In this subsection:AB298,2,331. “District board” has the meaning given in s. 38.01 (6).AB298,2,442. “Interactive critical mapping data” has the meaning given in sub. (3m) (ac).AB298,2,85(b) The office of school safety shall award grants to district boards to be used 6to submit interactive critical mapping data of the district boards’ technical college 7buildings to each local law enforcement agency with jurisdiction and to the office of 8school safety.AB298,2,129(c) The office of school safety shall accept applications for the grants under 10this subsection from the president of the technical college system board, who shall 11work jointly with the district boards and the local law enforcement agencies with 12jurisdiction to apply for the grants.AB298,2,2113(d) Grant moneys awarded under this subsection shall be used for site-specific 14interactive critical mapping data to enhance security and increase situational 15awareness for first responders. Any interactive critical mapping data created with 16grant money under this subsection shall be compatible with platforms and 17applications used by local, state, and federal public safety officials; may not require 18local, state, or federal public safety officials or district boards to purchase additional 19software; and shall include information that can best assist first responders in an 20emergency, such as building numbers, floors, suite designations, room numbers, or 21other available relevant location information.AB298,322Section 3. 165.88 (4) of the statutes is amended to read:AB298,3,223165.88 (4) Report. The department of justice shall submit an annual report 24to the cochairpersons of the joint committee on finance providing an account of the 1grants awarded under sub. subs. (2), (3m), and (3t) and the expenditures made with 2the grant moneys.AB298,3,33(end) Down Down /2025/related/proposals/ab298 true proposaltext /2025/related/proposals/ab298 proposaltext/2025/REG/AB298 proposaltext/2025/REG/AB298 section true Menu»2025»Related Documents»Proposal Text»AB298: Bill Text × Details for PDF view Link (Permanent link) Bookmark this location View toggle Go to top of document Search in this chapter Search in this section Search in this agency Search in this chapter group Search in this chapter Search in this section Cross references for section Acts affecting this section References to this 1970 Statutes Annotations Appellate Court Citations Administrative Code Index Reference lines Clear highlighting
