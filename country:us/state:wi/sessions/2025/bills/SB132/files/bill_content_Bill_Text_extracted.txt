Referred to Committee on Judiciary and Public Safety.SB132,1,41An Act to amend 946.70 (1) (a), (b), (c) and (d); to create 946.70 (1) (intro.) of 2the statutes; relating to: classification of the crime of impersonating law 3enforcement officers, fire fighters, and certain other emergency personnel and 4providing a penalty.Analysis by the Legislative Reference BureauUnder current law, a person may not impersonate a peace officer, a fire fighter, an emergency services medical practitioner, or an emergency medical provider with the intent to mislead others into believing that the person is actually an officer, a fire fighter, or emergency personnel. Current law classifies the crime as a Class A misdemeanor.
