Referred to Committee on Transportation and Local Government.SB202,1,21An Act to create 66.0601 (1) (d) of the statutes; relating to: local guaranteed 2income programs.Analysis by the Legislative Reference BureauThis bill prohibits a political subdivision from expending moneys of the political subdivision for the purpose of making payments to individuals under a guaranteed income program. “Guaranteed income program” is defined under the bill to mean a program under which individuals are provided with regular periodic cash payments that are unearned and that may be used for any purpose. Programs under which an individual is required to perform work or attend training are not “guaranteed income programs” under the bill.The people of the state of Wisconsin, represented in senate and assembly, do enact as follows:SB202,13Section 1. 66.0601 (1) (d) of the statutes is created to read:SB202,1,4466.0601 (1) (d) Guaranteed income program. 1. In this paragraph:SB202,2,25a. “Guaranteed income program” means a program under which individuals 6are provided with regular periodic cash payments that are unearned and that may 1be used for any purpose. “Guaranteed income program” does not include a program 2under which an individual is required to perform work or attend training.SB202,2,33b. “Political subdivision” means a city, village, town, or county.SB202,2,742. No political subdivision may expend moneys of the political subdivision, 5including shared revenue payments under subch.
