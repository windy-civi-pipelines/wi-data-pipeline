andAJR106,1,75Whereas, much like those who journeyed on the famed Mayflower over two 6centuries prior, the passengers aboard the Restauration sought religious freedom in 7the United States; andAJR106,1,108Whereas, the Restauration arrived in New York Harbor on October 9, 1825, to 9much sensation among the local press, with one newspaper headline describing the 10arrival of the Restauration as “a novel sight”;
