Referred to Committee on Criminal Justice and Public Safety.AB273,1,21An Act to amend 15.405 (5g) of the statutes; relating to: the membership of 2the Controlled Substances Board.Analysis by the Legislative Reference BureauUnder current law, the Controlled Substances Board, which performs various functions with regards to the scheduling and regulation of controlled substances and the Prescription Drug Monitoring Program, consists of nine members, including the chairpersons of the Pharmacy Examining Board, the Medical Examining Board, the Dentistry Examining Board, and the Board of Nursing. This bill adds the chairperson of the Physician Assistant Affiliated Credentialing Board to the membership of the board.The people of the state of Wisconsin, represented in senate and assembly, do enact as follows:AB273,13Section 1. 15.405 (5g) of the statutes is amended to read:AB273,2,6415.405 (5g) Controlled substances board.
