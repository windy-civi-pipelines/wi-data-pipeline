andAJR100,1,76Whereas, the black ribbon has been chosen as the symbol for this month to 7represent the sensitive and often stigmatized nature of these experiences; andAJR100,1,98Whereas, vaccine injuries may present gradually and are sometimes 9misattributed to other causes due to their subtle onset;
