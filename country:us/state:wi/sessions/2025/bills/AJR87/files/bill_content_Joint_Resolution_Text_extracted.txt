Referred to Committee on Rules.AJR87,1,21Relating to: proclaiming August and October 2025 to be Agent Orange Awareness 2Months in Wisconsin.AJR87,1,43Whereas, the United States was founded by those willing to endure enormous 4sacrifice so that individual and collective freedoms may be secured; andAJR87,1,65Whereas, Wisconsin was and is home to countless veterans of the armed forces 6who performed above and beyond the call of duty; andAJR87,1,107Whereas, throughout the years, service members have been called upon to 8take up arms against the enemies of the United States, and to risk their lives and 9leave behind their loved ones, for the purpose of honorably safeguarding the 10freedoms and liberties guaranteed to the nation's citizens; andAJR87,1,1411Whereas, over eight million such citizens bravely served in the Vietnam War, 12during a time when the herbicide known as “Agent 13 Orange” was widely used in 13Vietnam as part of the United States' Operation Ranch Hand, from 1961 to 1971; 14andAJR87,2,31Whereas, nearly 20 million gallons of this orange powder were sprayed over 2the land from helicopters or low-flying aircraft, exposing roughly 2.6 million United 3States service members to the herbicide and defoliant chemical; andAJR87,2,84Whereas, Agent Orange is a dioxin and carcinogenic chemical that enters the 5body through physical contact or ingestion and moves into the human cell nucleus, 6where it attacks the genes and causes a number of serious illnesses, including 7leukemia, lymphoma, myeloma, heart disease, soft tissue sarcoma, amyloidosis, 8diabetes, and various types of cancers; andAJR87,2,119Whereas, Agent Orange also causes genetic damage and, in some cases, the 10children and grandchildren of veterans exposed to Agent Orange have been born 11with spina bifida and other health issues; andAJR87,2,1512Whereas, while the names of fallen service members are listed upon the 13Vietnam Veterans Memorial in Washington, D.C., those veterans who fall victim to 14the effects of Agent Orange are not recognized as fatalities of the Vietnam War; now, 15therefore, be itAJR87,2,1916Resolved by the assembly, the senate concurring, That the Wisconsin 17Legislature formally recognizes both August and October 2025 as Agent Orange 18Awareness Months in Wisconsin in order to raise public awareness about Agent 19Orange and its impact on service members and their families.AJR87,2,2020(end) Down Down /2025/related/proposals/ajr87 true proposaltext /2025/related/proposals/ajr87 proposaltext/2025/REG/AJR87 proposaltext/2025/REG/AJR87 section true Menu»2025»Related Documents»Proposal Text»AJR87: Joint Resolution Text × Details for PDF view Link (Permanent link) Bookmark this location View toggle Go to top of document Search in this chapter Search in this section Search in this agency Search in this chapter group Search in this chapter Search in this section Cross references for section Acts affecting this section References to this 1970 Statutes Annotations Appellate Court Citations Administrative Code Index Reference lines Clear highlighting
