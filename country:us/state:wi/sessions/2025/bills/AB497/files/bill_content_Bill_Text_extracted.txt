Referred to Committee on Education.AB497,1,41An Act to amend 19.32 (1) and 19.82 (1); to create 19.36 (17), 115.7915 (6) (L), 2118.60 (2) (a) 10. and 119.23 (2) (a) 10. of the statutes; relating to: application 3of the public records and open meetings laws to charter schools and private 4schools participating in school choice programs.Analysis by the Legislative Reference BureauThis bill provides that charter schools and private schools participating in a parental choice program are subject to the state’s public records and open meetings laws. The bill includes an exemption from disclosure under the public records law for records concerning individual pupils that are maintained by such a school.The people of the state of Wisconsin, represented in senate and assembly, do enact as follows:AB497,15Section 1. 19.32 (1) of the statutes is amended to read:AB497,2,11619.32 (1) “Authority” means any of the following having custody of a record: a 7state or local office, elective official, agency, board, commission, committee, council, 1department or public body corporate and politic created by the constitution or by 2any law, ordinance, rule or order; a governmental or quasi-governmental 3corporation except for the Bradley center sports and entertainment corporation; a 4special purpose district; any court of law; the assembly or senate; a nonprofit 5corporation which receives more than 50 percent of its funds from a county or a 6municipality, as defined in s. 59.001 (3), and which provides services related to 7public health or safety to the county or municipality; a university police department 8under s. 175.42; a charter school, as defined in s. 115.001 (1), regardless of whether 9the charter school is or is determined to be an instrumentality of a school district 10under s. 118.40 (7); a private school participating in a program under s. 115.7915, 11118.60, or 119.23; or a formally constituted subunit of any of the foregoing.AB497,212Section 2. 19.36 (17) of the statutes is created to read:AB497,2,161319.36 (17) Certain pupil records.
