Referred to Committee on Rules.AJR92,1,11Relating to: recognizing the United States Marine Corps’s 250th birthday.AJR92,1,42Whereas, the history of the U.S. Marine Corps began with a vote taken by the 3Continental Congress on November 10, 1775, to raise two battalions of marines for 4service during the American Revolutionary War; andAJR92,1,65Whereas, the U.S. Marine Corps thus celebrates November 10 as its official 6birthday; andAJR92,1,87Whereas, November 10, 2025, will be the U.S. Marine Corps’s 250th birthday; 8andAJR92,1,119Whereas, in celebration of this historic date, we recognize the U.S. Marine 10Corps for its valiant and unwavering service to our country, constitution, and 11liberties for the past 250 years; andAJR92,1,1312Whereas, over 165,000 active-duty marines are currently stationed around the 13world to courageously defend our nation with unparalleled devotion; andAJR92,2,314Whereas, hundreds of marines have trained at Fort McCoy in Monroe County, 1where many hone their skills in live-fire exercises, medical training, and other 2activities, while some brave few demonstrate their remarkable grit in the Cold 3Weather Operations Course; andAJR92,2,54Whereas, every marine upholds and honors the U.S. Marine Corps’s motto of 5Semper Fidelis, “always faithful,” in their service to our nation and ideals; andAJR92,2,86Whereas, the people of Wisconsin sincerely appreciate the U.S. Marine 7Corps’s marines, veterans, and civilians for their sacrifices made in the name of the 8United States; andAJR92,2,109Whereas, in recognition of its uncompromising bravery and patriotism, the 10U.S. Marine Corps has our utmost confidence; now, therefore, be itAJR92,2,1311Resolved by the assembly, the senate concurring, That the Wisconsin 12Legislature celebrates the U.S. Marine Corps’s 250th birthday and recognizes its 13great history of service and achievement.AJR92,2,1414(end) Down Down /2025/related/proposals/ajr92 true proposaltext /2025/related/proposals/ajr92 proposaltext/2025/REG/AJR92 proposaltext/2025/REG/AJR92 section true Menu»2025»Related Documents»Proposal Text»AJR92: Joint Resolution Text × Details for PDF view Link (Permanent link) Bookmark this location View toggle Go to top of document Search in this chapter Search in this section Search in this agency Search in this chapter group Search in this chapter Search in this section Cross references for section Acts affecting this section References to this 1970 Statutes Annotations Appellate Court Citations Administrative Code Index Reference lines Clear highlighting
