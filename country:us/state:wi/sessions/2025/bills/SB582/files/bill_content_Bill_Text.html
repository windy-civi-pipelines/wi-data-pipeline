2025 SENATE BILL 582
October 24, 2025 - Introduced by Senators Cabral-Guevara, Hutton, Jacque and Wanggaard, cosponsored by Representatives Sortwell, Donovan, Brill, Dittrich, Goeben, Green, B. Jacobson, Knodl, Kreibich, Melotik, O'Connor and Wichgers. Referred to Committee on Education.
SB582,1,4
1An Act to create 118.60 (3) (e) and 119.23 (3) (c) of the statutes; relating to:
2the pupil application process to attend a private school under a parental choice
3program and modifying and creating administrative rules related to pupil
4applications to participate in parental choice programs.
Analysis by the Legislative Reference Bureau
Under this bill, for purposes of determining whether a pupil is eligible to continue participating in the Milwaukee Parental Choice Program, the Racine Parental Choice Program, or the Wisconsin Parental Choice Program (parental choice program), the Department of Public Instruction is prohibited from requiring documentation to verify the pupil’s residence unless the pupil’s residence has changed since documentation was last used to verify the pupil’s residence. The bill also updates DPI’s administrative rules related to pupil eligibility for the parental choice programs to be consistent with changes made in the bill.
The people of the state of Wisconsin, represented in senate and assembly, do enact as follows:
