Referred to Committee on Senate Organization.SJR17,1,11Relating to: honoring April 22, 2025, as the 55th anniversary of Earth Day.SJR17,1,42Whereas, Earth Day is an annual event celebrated around the world on April 322 to demonstrate support for environmental conservation while recognizing the 4protection of our land, water, and air; andSJR17,1,75Whereas, Earth Day was founded by the U.S. Senator Gaylord Nelson of 6Wisconsin, who called for a “national teach-in on the crisis of the environment” to 7be held on April 22, 1970; andSJR17,1,108Whereas, Senator Nelson aimed to send a message to Washington, DC, from 9people across the nation that public opinion called for clean water and air and the 10protection of our environment; andSJR17,1,1311Whereas, the first Earth Day motivated 20 million Americans, including 2,000 12colleges, to organize together to advocate for a diverse array of solutions to climate 13challenges; andSJR17,2,414Whereas, Senator Nelson’s leadership and the impacts of the first Earth Day 1went on to produce the Environmental Decade, which ushered in the support of 2environmental legislation such as the National Environmental Policy Act, the 3Endangered Species Act, and stringent amendments to strengthen the Clean Air 4and Clean Water Acts; andSJR17,2,75Whereas, Earth Day is now celebrated in 193 countries around the world, 6creating opportunities for civic engagement and community collaboration to leave a 7more prosperous natural environment for generations to come; andSJR17,2,118Whereas, April 22, 2025, is the 55th anniversary of Earth Day and events will 9take place across Wisconsin and worldwide to encourage actions on behalf of our 10planet to safeguard our natural resources and protect our environment; now, 11therefore, be itSJR17,2,1512Resolved by the senate, the assembly concurring, That the members of 13the Wisconsin Legislature honor April 22, 2025, as the 55th anniversary of Earth 14Day and encourage Wisconsinites to continue actions to preserve our environment 15for all to enjoy.SJR17,2,1616(end) Down Down /2025/related/proposals/sjr17 true proposaltext /2025/related/proposals/sjr17 proposaltext/2025/REG/SJR17 proposaltext/2025/REG/SJR17 section true Menu»2025»Related Documents»Proposal Text»SJR17: Joint Resolution Text × Details for PDF view Link (Permanent link) Bookmark this location View toggle Go to top of document Search in this chapter Search in this section Search in this agency Search in this chapter group Search in this chapter Search in this section Cross references for section Acts affecting this section References to this 1970 Statutes Annotations Appellate Court Citations Administrative Code Index Reference lines Clear highlighting
