Referred to Committee on Health, Aging and Long-Term Care.AB68,1,21An Act to amend 961.385 (2) (cs) 1., 961.385 (5) (b) and 961.385 (6) (a) of the 2statutes; relating to: the prescription drug monitoring program.Analysis by the Legislative Reference BureauCurrent law requires the Controlled Substances Board to establish a prescription drug monitoring program (PDMP) that requires pharmacies and health care practitioners to generate records documenting the dispensing of monitored prescription drugs. Records from the PDMP may be disclosed to persons specified under current law or by the board by rule. This bill makes changes to certain requirements under the PDMP:1. Subject to exceptions, current law requires that a patient’s records under the program be reviewed before a practitioner issues a prescription order for the patient. However, current law provides that this requirement does not apply after April 1, 2025. The bill extends the application of this requirement by five years, to April 1, 2030.2. Current law requires the board to conduct a quarterly review of the PDMP to evaluate the actual outcomes of the PDMP compared with projected outcomes. However, current law provides that this requirement does not apply after October 30, 2025. The bill extends the application of this requirement by five years, to October 30, 2030.For further information see the state fiscal estimate, which will be printed as an appendix to this bill.The people of the state of Wisconsin, represented in senate and assembly, do enact as follows:AB68,11Section 1. 961.385 (2) (cs) 1. of the statutes is amended to read:AB68,2,62961.385 (2) (cs) 1. Require that a patient’s records under the program be 3reviewed before the practitioner issues a prescription order for the patient. The 4review required under this subdivision may be performed by the practitioner or by 5the practitioner’s agent in accordance with applicable standards of practice. This 6subdivision does not apply after April 1, 2025 2030.AB68,27Section 2. 961.385 (5) (b) of the statutes is amended to read:AB68,2,88961.385 (5) (b) This subsection does not apply after October 30, 2025 2030.AB68,39Section 3. 961.385 (6) (a) of the statutes is amended to read:AB68,2,1110961.385 (6) (a) The results of the board’s review under sub. (5).
